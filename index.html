Privacy Policy for Spectrum Aware

Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Privacy Commitment: Spectrum Aware is committed to protecting your privacy. We believe in transparency and your right to control your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in our application.

1. Information We Collect

IMPORTANT: All information collected by Spectrum Aware is stored EXCLUSIVELY on your device. We do not store, transmit, or have access to any of your data outside of your phone or app. Your data never leaves your device except when YOU choose to share a PDF report.

1.1 Personal Information

Spectrum Aware collects minimal personal information to provide its core functionality:

  • User Name: Used solely to personalize your experience within the app and identify different user profiles.
  • Age: Used to provide age-appropriate questionnaires and assessments.

Privacy Guarantee: This personal information (name and age) is stored only on your device and never leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to share a PDF report that contains this information.

1.2 Assessment Data

The app stores the following information related to assessments:

  • Questionnaire responses
  • Assessment results and scores
  • Historical assessment data for tracking trends
  • Generated PDF reports

1.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

Important: We do not collect, access, transmit, or store:

  • Your email address or phone number
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (beyond what's required for ads)
  • Photos or media files
  • Contacts or calendar information
  • Any personally identifiable information beyond name and age

2. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All of your data is stored exclusively on your device. This includes:

  • User profiles (name and age)
  • All questionnaire responses
  • Assessment results and history
  • Generated reports

3.2 No Cloud Storage

We do not:

  • Upload your data to any servers
  • Store your data in the cloud
  • Transmit your assessment data over the internet
  • Share your data with any third parties (except as noted for ads)

3.3 Data Security

Your data security is ensured by:

  • Local-only storage on your device protected by your device's security features
  • No network transmission of personal or assessment data
  • Industry-standard security practices in app development

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

Spectrum Aware displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Important details:

  • Ad Serving: Ads are served by Google's AdMob platform
  • Data Collection by AdMob: Google may collect certain information for ad delivery, including:
    • Device information (type, operating system)
    • Ad interaction data
    • Advertising identifiers
  • No Personal Data Sharing: We do not share any of your personal information, names, ages, or assessment data with AdMob or any advertising networks
  • Google's Privacy Policy: For information on how Google collects and processes data, please review Google's Privacy Policy

4.2 Ad Personalization

You can control ad personalization through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.

Your data is never shared with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • User-Initiated Sharing: When you choose to share or export a PDF report, you control where it goes
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, legal process, or government request
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

6. Children's Privacy

Spectrum Aware is designed to help assess developmental patterns in children and adults. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond the minimal data (name and age) needed for app functionality
  • All data remains on the device and is controlled by the parent or guardian
  • We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) requirements
  • Parents/guardians have full control over their child's data and can delete it at any time

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app at any time
  • Modification: Edit user profiles and data through the app settings
  • Deletion: Delete individual profiles, assessments, or all app data through the app's "Delete All Data" feature
  • Export: Generate and share PDF reports containing your assessment data
  • App Uninstall: Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored data from your device

8. Data Retention

Your data is retained as follows:

  • On Your Device: Indefinitely, until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • On Our Servers: Not applicable - we don't store your data on servers
  • Backups: Any backups you create are under your control

9. International Users

Spectrum Aware can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • No data crosses international borders through our systems
  • Your data jurisdiction is determined by your physical location
  • GDPR rights apply to users in the European Economic Area
